About Ravenloft
Curse of Strahd is a gothic horror campaign set in Ravenloft, a demiplane of the shadow realm, also known as the Shadowfell. Here, Strahd resides, ruling over his domain. An oppressive sorrow hangs over the denizen's of this realm, their tired faces sapped of hopes and ambitions, as they live out their days, trapped by the mists, in a land where the sun has ceased to rise.

Dungeon Master
Featuring Cordial as the dungeon master, this campaign has high ambitions for intense melodrama and detailed character interaction. With over a year of consistent experience in this role, this will be her first take at a published horror campaign. She hopes to encourage dynamicy, character development, and complex inter-PC and inter-NPC relationships. When she finishes getting experience running this horrific module, she plans to run it semi-professionally.
